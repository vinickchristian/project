Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Photographism

Reason: The name "Photographism" combines "photographic" and "graphism," reflecting the blend of photography and graphic design. This name is relevant as it showcases both photographic and graphic design elements, highlighting the unique and artistic approach to visual content creation.

Site Purpose

Purpose: The website will showcase the photographer's portfolio, offer photography services, and provide a means for potential clients to contact the photographer for inquiries and bookings.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A visitor is looking to hire a photographer for their upcoming wedding. They want to see samples of previous wedding photos and find information on pricing and availability.
  • Scenario 2: An art enthusiast is interested in purchasing prints of the photographer's work. They want to know what types of prints are available and how they can place an order.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #333333 (used for headings and navigation)

Accent Color: #f4a261 (used for buttons and links)

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings)
